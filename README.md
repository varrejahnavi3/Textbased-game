# Textbased-game
The main objective of the project is to create an interface for students where they can sell or 
buy the used textbooks. It is mandatory for the students to login to the website before selling 
or buying a product. Students can search for the book based on title, author and subject. Once 
they find the desired book they can contact the seller offering that book and get it from the 
seller by pay and get method in the campus itself. Using this system the students can purchase 
a book or a stationary item like calculators, drafters etc. easily instead of going out to a book 
store wasting both time and money. Seller will give the description of the book and the selling 
price along with his contact details. Students may not be the sellers every time they may 
exchange the books or donate books. The books will be categorized based on the subject and 
its stream.
